Pro Tips

Orbit Mode: Switch to orbit mode for automatic orbital velocity calculation when placing bodies.
GPU Acceleration: Enable GPU mode in Performance tab for faster simulations with many bodies.
Save Configurations: Use presets to quickly load interesting solar system configurations.
Energy Monitoring: Watch the Energy tab to see conservation of energy in action.
